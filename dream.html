Dream Big (A Realistic Guide To Success)


NOTE FROM WRITER: To all readers of this blog thanks for supporting me in my previous blog, and i want to thank all my friends to be a great support to me in all my hard times and making me smile and be happy always. Thank you all.

Dreams, the one thing anyone in this world can do even if you don’t have anything. But the sad reality is people stopped dreaming bigger or even more specifically they abandoned bigger dreams and settle for mediocre dreams. The very first step to success is to dream which almost all people have failed today except a very few. I’m not saying that I was doing great, even I settled for mediocrity until I realised the hurtful truth, which is the life won’t be kind to you all the time.

If you dream of, say getting a good job at a good company I assure, you will get a good job at a good company. But now comes the truth which people tend to object or scared to see which is your life will be done in there. There was this wonderful quote which not a lie, as far as to me its truth “If you dream of making a hundred million dollars you will make only hundred million dollars, you won’t make hundred and ten million dollars”. This is because once you are settled with your dreams, you mind won’t let you face any more challenges. It creates an impression that my goal is to get a hundred million dollars and I got it I don’t need more money than this. This quote applies for all.

So now if you are a student say you want sixty marks out of hundred in your exams and if you work hard but with a mindset to get sixty marks guess what you will only get sixty marks. So the point I coming to converge at is to make your dreams bigger never settle for some small and mediocre aim for the bigger things.

Now a days another big mistake people do is to plan everything. When ever I see someone plan something about their life I not making fun of anyone but I tend to laugh. The thing which people are not understanding is tomorrow is never certain. You might live a joyful life today but tomorrow something may happen. I not trying to make you guys a pessimist rather I’m making you guys to understand the reality and adapt to it. Live your life today rather worrying about tomorrow. Start woking today rather saying I’ll do it tomorrow. That tomorrow may not come. Make your dreams big always aim high and don’t let the noise of other people suppress your minds voice. Dream big and achieve in life.

Thank you.

See you guys soon.
