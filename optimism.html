Optimism


NOTE FROM WRITER: To all readers of this blog thanks for supporting me in my previous blog, and i want to thank all my friends to be a great support to me in all my hard times and making me smile and be happy always. Thank you all.

People sometimes say that their life is miserable, terrible and they always complain about their life. So for those people its all your thoughts which makes you feel that way. I would like to say a small story.

“ Once upon a time, there was this two villages, the village in the mountains and the village in the valley. One day a man was shifting his house from the village in the mountains to the village in the valleys, while he was travelling he saw an old monk farming in his field, the man goes next to him and says hello I am coming from the village in the mountains and I am moving to the village in the valleys and then he asks how will the life in the valley be. The monk asks him how was your life in the mountains ? The man says it was TERRIBLE, I was staying in a shitty place and I ate a weird stew which tasted like nothing and exclaims it was horrible. The old monk now replies ‘IT WILL BE THE SAME’. The man got devastated.

Now an another man was shifting from the village in the mountains to the valley and on his way he sees the old monk farming his field he goes to him and asks the same question and the old monk asks how was your life there ? He relied it so awesome and I loved every single moment of it. I experienced the life of the people in the village and I loved the cool temperature and I ate home cooked stew and my stay was so good. The old monk now replies ‘IT WILL BE THE SAME’. The man so happy singing a dancing through out his journey and lived a wonderful life in the village in the valley ”

Now, from the above story its crystal clear that ones life will always depend on their thoughts. And always having a positive thought can improve your life by very high means. There are only two types of people in this world;

Optimists, people who are like I loved it , I can do it, I will try my very best etc.

Pessimist, people who are life Can I do it, will I be able to it, it will be hard etc.

It’s proven that optimistic people are most likely to achieve success in their lives and in the same way only having positive thoughts alone doesn’t guarantee success, with positive thoughts you have to work for it. And also not on all cases positive thoughts works, for example you can’t climb Mt.Everest in one day even if you have tons and tons of positive thought. Lol.

Every morning when you wake up you have to have two thoughts;

That something good is going to happen to before the day ends.

If that does not happen i’ll make it happen.

“ I never forget.
Not for you one day.
I knew I’d find you in the end.
Its our destiny”
