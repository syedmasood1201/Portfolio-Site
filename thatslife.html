That’s Life


NOTE FROM WRITER: To all readers of this blog thanks for supporting me in my previous blog, and i want to thank all my friends to be a great support to me in all my hard times and making me smile and be happy always. Thank you all.

Sometimes people come into your life and you know right away that they were meant to be there, to serve some sort of purpose, teach you a lesson, change your character, take you in right path, or to help you figure out who you are and who you want to become. You never know who these people may be (possibly your roommate, neighbor, coworker, or even a complete stranger) but when you lock your eyes with them, you know at that very moment that they will make you life more great than you expect.

I suggest you guys to have a good friend who is not only a very good companion when you do crazy stuffs, but also to have a friend who helps you overcome when you are in a state of confusion or any other means. And sometimes things happen to you that may seem horrible, painful, and unfair at first, but in reflection you find out that without those obstacles you would have never realized your potential, strength, willpower, or heart.

Everything happens for a reason. Nothing happens by means of chance or luck. Illness, injury, love, lost moments of true greatness, and sheer stupidity all occur to test the limits of your soul. Without these small tests, whatever they may be, life would be like a smoothly paved, straight flat road, to nowhere. It would be safe and comfortable, but dull and utterly pointless.

The people who you meet affect your life, and success and downfalls you experience help to create who you become. Even the bad experiences can be learned from. In fact, they are probably the most poignant and important ones. If someone hurts you, betrays you, or breaks you heart, forgive them , for they have helped you to learn about trust and important of being cautious when you open your heart. If someone loves you, love them back unconditionally, not only because they love you, but because in a way, they are teaching you to love and open your heart and eyes to things.

And in order to achieve something in life make your self concentrated at that particular thing. Don’t let your mind to get diverted, I m not saying you guys to fully avoid the society and stay alone, that’s not the true way to live life. Life is meant to be lived not to be wasted it must be lived in a way which is filled with happiness and joy.

Make every day count!!! Appreciate every moment and take from those moments everything that you possibly can for you may never be able to experience it again. Talk to people that you have never talked to , and actually listen. Let yourself fall in love, break free, and set your sights high. Hold your head because you have every right to. Tell yourself you are a great individual and believe in yourself, for if you don’t believe in yourself, it will be hard for others to believe in you. You can make your life anything you wish. Create your own life then go out and live it with absolutely no regrets.

Thank you all once again..
