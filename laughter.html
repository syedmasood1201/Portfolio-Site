Laughter and Happiness in lifee


NOTE FROM WRITER: To all readers of this blog thanks for supporting me in my previous blog, and i want to thank all my friends to be a great support to me in all my hard times and making me smile and be happy always. Thank you all.

The only thing no one can ever steal from a person is laughter and happiness. Yes, and laughter plays a huge role in our lives, but the one thing we all do without fail is forget how to live and started to make very very complex solutions for simple problems for which we have the simplest solution in front of us. I know it may sound confusing but the sad reality is we do it. We all may heard of a thing long back called socialising which is almost nearing extinction stage. I personally believe that socialising with people makes a very big impact on a persons life. It helps him find new people, those new people may be your new best friend or your love of life who knows what happens and when it happens.

But now a days people in this technologically developing world, think the whole point of happiness is to be online, gaming, texting and seeing stupid videos, they believe that that’s what gives them happiness and makes them laugh and make their lives bright. But the reality is, no its not. If you look just an inch or two above your five or six inch device you a see a far beautiful world out there waiting for you to explore screaming to you, to make you smile. I personally understood this very recently and I was like “Oh! God. I wasted a lot of my years”. I assure you if you be the one as I mentioned earlier at any one point in your life you will surely regret it. People may say work hard now and spend you old age happily travelling the world or doing things you wish you would have done in your young age, that is the most worst advise in the entire dictionary of advise, the reality is it does not happen. Maybe, a few cases are exceptional, but you will regret I’m sure about that. You have to learn to live your life, laugh, be happy and fell in love with it. People think life is long I can do that anytime but I’m sorry to break this to you, no its not, life is very short to be lethargic once it’s gone its gone.

Try to be more lively, express yourself meet a lot of new people talk a lot to people, find new friends, but be wise don’t be foolish and fell in some wrong company that can ruin your life. Start saving from a very young age and use that money to be happy. Experience the actual point of living. Travel to places, learn new languages, explore other cultures. Every time you are travelling to a new place you should have a feeling of happiness and a piece of achievement. The most important part is don’t stop or control your laughter, laugh as much as you like. Be happy as much as you wish. Remember money can’t buy you happiness it can give a gist about happiness but that’s not happiness at all. The key thing to be happy is to be kind, be kind to others and then see the respect and the warmth you get that is what real happiness is. Spread happiness around you as much as you can. Start admiring, not only about the biggest ones even the smaller things, don’t try to be another person by faking your real personality just to fit in cool kids gang, instead be yourself even if your different that other I assure you, you will be happy and people will definitely like you, not for you being cool but for being unique, for being yourself. Every one like to be around a happy person, the one who can make them laugh or make them happy even in the most difficult times, no one likes to hang around a fake guy who personifies himself as a cool and happy guy.

You can’t be happy or make a person happy or to smile by being someone else, who ever it may be. Be you, be unique, be more lively. Laughter and happiness even makes you feel younger. Live you life as life don’t be machine which does a something repeatedly be realistic.

“You don’t stop laughing because you grow older, You grow older because you stop laughing”

Maurice Chevalier
