A Boring Story


NOTE FROM WRITER: To all readers of this blog thanks for supporting me in my previous blog, and i want to thank all my friends to be a great support to me in all my hard times and making me smile and be happy always. Thank you all.

Every one gets bored, king to soldier, rich to poor, young to old, but people fail to accept the actual point of boredom. Bored people usually become crazy they start to be lazy, watch some crappy series or shows and some may even write a blog with the name “A Boring Story”. Boredom differs from people to people, it mainly depends on the people you are with the things that you do when bored, they speak a lot about your character. I have read people doing some worst things which cause harm for them and a very tragic situation to many and at the next hand i have seen a few doing things which people may think as wonders.

Boredom is actually a state of feeling disinterested in ones surroundings, boredom usually affects teenagers a lot. There are a lot of things people can do to overcome boredom, usually I suggest to take a walk be calm and sit under a tree or some place which is peaceful and start to understand things start to feel your surroundings, notice the unnoticed and feel the nature and its beauty, start to appreciate even the smallest thing possible, our single appreciation can mean a lot to someone.

There is a whole world left unexplored, give a break to social media and talk to people, talk to your friends, not only your friends it may be any one who you like to talk with. Spend your life usefully, enjoy every moment of it. Don’t burn your daylight being lazy. Start being productive, life is meant to be lived and with no doubt life is beautiful and full of celebrations, friendship, emotions and love. The most fun part to not get bored in life is to always be ready to face the adversity and challenges. And don’t over experience life because there are difficult situation in life as well so be careful when you are experiencing life because you might get hurt too hard, also at times life will be too hard to handle and it may be selfish.

And another most important thing to remember to avoid being or getting bored is to be optimistic trust me it helps a lot. Being optimistic not only reduces boredom but also increases your self confidence to a maximum level, but even being too optimistic can hurt badly, for instance you can’t climb Mount.Everest in one day even you have tons and tons of positive thoughts it wont be possible, lol.

So try to be more lively express yourself, live life as life, try appreciating things, enjoy every second of your life with friends and family, because nothing in this world will be everlasting. Don’t let your boredom take off all the fun, happiness and emotions of your life from you.

THANK YOU ALL ONCE AGAIN
