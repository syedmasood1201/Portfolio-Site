Climate change and global warming, the two term which I hope we hear these a lot but still we tend to maintain an act of silence. People may think why should I care? And be lethargic but the sad truth is maybe not you but I’m sure your kids will suffer. I know it’s sounds pretty harsh but the reality is always harsh and hard to digest. People often come up with more complex ideas for such simple problems the funny part is we have way more easier solutions lying in front of us. Many people now a days talk a lot about saving the environment and raising their voice again climatic changes and global warming, but I’m sad to tell this that nothing is gonna happen just by talking about it. People have to start taking initiatives to actually reduce global warming. I also want to mention this quote which turned me in right path and thought me the reality and that is;

“The world changes by your examples,

Not by your opinion”


So instead of just talking about the problems which we all already know we have, let’s try to find some simple, innovative most importantly effective solutions to suppress climatic changes and global warming and save this world from a catastrophe or the worst case which is an extinction level event (ELI) . So first of all it becomes increasingly important to know what are these terms, and what do they actually mean and what effects does it have, on this earth, humans and other living beings.

Global warming is the long term raise of the average or global temperature of the earths climatic system. Green house gases tend to increase global warming by very high means. There are a lot of green house gases which increase global warming which ultimately leads to climates changes and mass destruction.

First let’s talk about the most important of these all which is CO2. The Atmospheric CO2 concentration has increased by 3 times in the last decade. This is one of the important factor for climatic changes. The logs shows us the significant increase in CO2 concentration clearly.

In 1950, the atmospheric CO2 concentration is recorded as 300 ppm (parts per million) and the atmospheric CO2 concentration in 1960, is recorded as 316 ppm (parts per million). These recorded values is not that much threatening, the CO2 released will be compensated by the CO2 consumption by the plants and hence the cycle goes on like that. And later days went and a lot of factors which mainly includes industrialisation, infrastructure development, agricultural expansions, etc. Which is not at all bad. If all those got along together with nature or in even simpler terms not causing any harm to nature, but sadly it lead to the destruction of forests or a more standard term which is deforestation.

Now with these statistics can you guys assume the atmospheric CO2 concentration in 2017? Most of your assumptions might be wrong because the CO2 concentrations started to rise alarming in massive peaks . The CO2 values breaches the 400 ppm (parts per million) threshold in 2014. The growth rate rose to 2.2 ppm (parts per million) per year during last decade.

The atmospheric CO2 concentration recorded in 2017 is 406.58 ppm (parts per million) which is a 90.58 % increase in CO2 concentration. Now can you guys predict or assume the CO2 concentration of the hottest year ever recorded? It was 2016 with a CO2 concentration of 403.3 ppm (parts per million) the increase in the concentration is calculated to be 3.3 ppm (parts per million) which is 50% more than the past decade. Accordingly in 2018 the atmospheric CO2 concentration was recorded as 407.4 ppm (parts per million) which is another big jump in the co2 concentration. It didn’t stop there, the recorded concentration of atmospheric CO2 in 2019 is 411.2 ppm (parts per million). You may think that theres just a small change in the numbers, but believe that small change in numbers affects the worlds climate and temperature a lot. So the difference in the concentration was calculated as 3.9 ppm (parts per million) as I mentioned it might seem as a small number but believe me that’s massive. And this concentration difference is also the 2nd highest annual jump in atmospheric CO2 concentration. Another data to be mentioned here is the concentration of CO2 as if on June 4, 2019 is 414.7 ppm (parts per million) and but this rate the atmospheric co2 concentration will reach an alarming level of 500 ppm (parts per million) very soon if it goes on like this maybe this century, so this concentration increase leads to the increase in the earths temperature by 5.4° F. Which creates a massive climatic change. Not only it crates a massive climatic change it also initiates a lot of catastrophic events as I said or even the worst of all an Extinction level event (ELE). These events does not affect any particular region, instead it affects the whole world. And all these catastrophic events causes mass destruction of major cities and life forms and at one particular point of time it ultimately leads to an Extinction level Event or ELE in short. Which I hope no one wants it to come true. In the next article will see about the major and very serious events or changes which occurs due to climatic changes and global warming.
